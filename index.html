Gesture guide
🤏
Pinch — Draw / Place
Bring thumb + index together to draw, or confirm a shape start/end point.
✌️
Hover — Aim cursor
Separate fingers — a glowing ink-drop cursor tracks your index tip.
🖐️
Open palm — Erase / Wipe
All fingers spread erases locally. Hold 2 s to wipe the active layer.
✊
Fist — Cancel shape
Close all fingers to cancel a shape currently being drawn.
